About Ghazala

A journey from physics to frontend development

My Story

My journey began at Hewad School, where I first discovered my love for learning and problem-solving. This foundation led me to pursue physics at Herat University, where I spent two fascinating years exploring the fundamental laws that govern our universe.

The analytical thinking and mathematical precision I learned in physics became the perfect foundation for my transition into the digital world. At Code to Inspire, I discovered my passion for graphics and motion design, learning how to bring static ideas to life through visual storytelling.

Currently, I'm expanding my skills as a frontend development student at CTA, where I'm learning to bridge the gap between design and functionality. My recent certification in HTML and CSS from UC Berkeley has given me a solid foundation in web technologies.

"From understanding the physics of light to capturing it through photography, from designing motion graphics to coding interactive experiences - every skill builds upon the last."
